<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Diesel Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multipoint Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li><li>Common Rail Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Throttle Body Injection is a type of fuel injection system that injects fuel into the throttle body, which then mixes with air before entering the engine. Multipoint Injection involves multiple injectors placed at each intake port of the engine to deliver fuel more precisely. Direct Injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for better fuel efficiency and performance. Common Rail Injection uses a high-pressure fuel rail to deliver fuel to individual injectors for more efficient combustion. These different types of injector systems cater to varying needs and requirements in the automotive market.</p></p>
