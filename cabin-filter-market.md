<p><p>Normal type cabin filters are basic air filters that are installed in vehicles to block out dust, pollen, and other particles from entering the cabin. On the other hand, activated carbon series filters are an upgraded version of cabin filters that also reduce odors and harmful gases by using activated carbon material. Both types are widely available in the market to cater to different customer preferences and needs.</p></p>
