<p><p>Oil & Gas Risers & Flowlines market consists of two main types of infrastructure essential for the transportation of oil and gas from deep water drilling operations. Risers are vertical pipes that connect the seabed to the surface platform, allowing for the safe transfer of hydrocarbons. Flowlines, on the other hand, are horizontal pipes that transport the oil and gas from the platform to the processing facilities onshore. Both risers and flowlines play a crucial role in the efficient extraction and transportation of oil and gas resources.</p></p>
