<p><p>Mineral Fiber Acoustic Panels come in various types including Glasswool, Stonewool, Rockwool, Slagwool, Glass Filaments, Ceramic Fibers, and other materials. Glasswool is made from recycled glass and is known for its lightweight nature. Stonewool is made from volcanic rock and is known for its high fire resistance. Rockwool is made from basalt rock and provides excellent thermal insulation. Slagwool is made from blast furnace slag and is valued for its sound absorption qualities. Glass Filaments are made from spun glass fibers and are used for reinforcement in composites. Ceramic Fibers are made from alumina-silica and are known for their high temperature resistance. Other materials used in Mineral Fiber Acoustic Panels may include perlite, vermiculite, and mineral wool.</p></p>
