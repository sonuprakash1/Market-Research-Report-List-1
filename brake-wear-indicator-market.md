<p><ul><li>Electrical Indicator</li><li>Audible Indicator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Brake wear indicators come in two main types: electrical indicators and audible indicators. Electrical indicators are sensors that detect when brake pads are worn down to a certain level, triggering a warning light on the dashboard. Audible indicators, on the other hand, produce a squealing sound when the brake pads are close to needing replacement. Both types of indicators provide drivers with a clear signal that it is time to have their brakes serviced, ensuring safety on the road.</p></p>
