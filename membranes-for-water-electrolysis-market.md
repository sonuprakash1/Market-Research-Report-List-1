<p><strong>The Membranes for Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proton Exchange Membranes</li><li>Ion Exchange Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membranes are essential components in water electrolysis processes, separating the electrodes and enabling the flow of ions to produce hydrogen and oxygen. Proton Exchange Membranes facilitate the exchange of protons while blocking other molecules, ideal for applications requiring high efficiency. Ion Exchange Membranes, on the other hand, selectively allow specific ions to pass through, making them suitable for varying electrolysis processes. Both membrane types play crucial roles in the water electrolysis market, offering unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
