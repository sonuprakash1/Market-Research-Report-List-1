<p><p>Tellurium Dioxide (TeO2) is commonly available in two main types: α-TeO2 and β-TeO2. The α-TeO2 form is known for its stability and resistance to temperature changes, making it ideal for use in various optical and electronic applications. On the other hand, the β-TeO2 form is characterized by its high refractive index and birefringence properties, making it suitable for use in optical waveguides and other specialized applications. Both types of Tellurium Dioxide play a crucial role in the electronics and optics industries.</p></p>
