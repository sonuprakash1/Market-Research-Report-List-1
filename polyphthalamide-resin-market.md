<p><strong>The Polyphthalamide Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unfilled PPA</li><li>Glass Fiber</li><li>Carbon Fiber</li><li>Mineral Filled</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyphthalamide Resin market can be categorized into various types based on their composition and properties. Unfilled PPA refers to the pure form of resin without any additional fillers, while Glass Fiber PPA contains added glass fibers for enhanced strength and stiffness. Carbon Fiber PPA incorporates carbon fibers for increased durability and heat resistance. Mineral Filled PPA utilizes minerals for improved wear resistance. Lastly, Hybrid PPA combines different fillers to achieve a balance of properties such as strength, heat resistance, and abrasion resistance.</p></p>
