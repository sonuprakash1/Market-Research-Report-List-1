<p><ul><li>UV Bandpass</li><li>VIS Bandpass</li><li>IR Bandpass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bandpass filters are used in various industries to allow only specific wavelengths of light to pass through while blocking others. UV bandpass filters are designed to transmit ultraviolet (UV) light and block all other wavelengths. VIS bandpass filters transmit visible light, allowing only a specific range of colors to pass through. IR bandpass filters are used to transmit infrared (IR) light while blocking unwanted wavelengths. These filters cater to different applications such as UV sterilization, fluorescence microscopy, spectroscopy, and thermal imaging, depending on the specific wavelength requirements.</p></p>
