<p><p>Passenger Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market includes two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a Hall effect to detect changes in magnetic fields, providing accurate speed readings. Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize magnets and electric currents to measure wheel speed. Both types are crucial in modern vehicles for functions like anti-lock braking systems and traction control. The market for these sensors is driven by technological advancements in vehicle safety systems and the increasing demand for improved performance and efficiency in passenger vehicles.</p></p>
