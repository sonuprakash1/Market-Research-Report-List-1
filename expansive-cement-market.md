<p><strong>The Expansive Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>K Type</li><li>M Type</li><li>S Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Expansive cement is categorized into three main types- K Type, M Type, and S Type. K Type is known for its high early strength and rapid setting, making it suitable for quick construction projects. M Type offers moderate expansion capabilities and is often used in structures requiring controlled expansion. S Type provides slow expansion and is commonly used in massive concrete structures. Each type of expansive cement caters to specific project requirements, offering unique properties like rapid setting, controlled expansion, and slow expansion to meet various construction needs.</p></p>
