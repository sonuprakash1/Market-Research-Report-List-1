<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Offshore Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore pipes market consists of three main types: ERW pipes, LSAW pipes, and SSAW pipes. ERW pipes, or Electric Resistance Welded pipes, are manufactured using high-frequency electrical currents to weld the edges, resulting in a strong and seamless pipe. LSAW pipes, or Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded pipes, are made by bending and welding a steel plate, offering higher resistance and durability. SSAW pipes, or Spiral Submerged Arc Welded pipes, are produced through a spiral welding process, providing strength and flexibility. These different pipe types cater to various offshore applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
