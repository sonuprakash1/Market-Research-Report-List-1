<p><p>PET self-adhesive tapes come in two main types: water-based adhesive tape and oil-based adhesive tape. Water-based adhesive tape is environmentally friendly, easy to use, and leaves no residue when removed. It is commonly used in packaging, labeling, and decorative applications. On the other hand, oil-based adhesive tape offers strong adhesion and durability, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications such as construction and industrial settings. Both types of tapes offer unique benefits and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
