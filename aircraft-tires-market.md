<p><p>Aircraft tires market can be divided into two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have ply cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, giving them greater flexibility and stability during landing and takeoff. Bias tires, on the other hand, have cords that run diagonally across the tire, making them more durable and resistant to damage. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different aircraft depending on factors such as load capacity and speed requirements.</p></p>
