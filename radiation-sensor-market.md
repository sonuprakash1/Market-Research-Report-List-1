<p><strong>The Radiation Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CSP</li><li>CerPin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation sensor market types include CSP (Compact Solid-State Photon) and CerPin (Ceramic Pin-Type). CSP sensors are compact, lightweight, and offer high sensitivity to detect gamma radiation in various applications such as environmental monitoring and medical imaging. On the other hand, CerPin sensors are ceramic-based pin-type sensors that provide accurate measurement of gamma radiation levels in harsh environments, making them ideal for nuclear power plants and industrial facilities. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and ensuring safety from radiation exposure.</p></p>
