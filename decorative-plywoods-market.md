<p><p>Decorative plywoods are categorized into three main types based on their thickness: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywoods below 6mm are commonly used for projects that require a lightweight material with a smooth finish, such as cabinet backs or drawer bottoms. The 6mm-18mm plywoods are versatile and commonly used for furniture making, interior decorations, and DIY projects. Plywoods above 18mm are preferred for heavy-duty applications like flooring, roofing, and structural support due to their enhanced strength and durability.</p></p>
