<p><p>Pipeline tracer wires are used for locating and tracking pipelines underground. There are two main types of tracer wires - pure copper conductor and copper clad steel conductor. Pure copper conductor wires are made entirely of copper, offering high conductivity and resistance to corrosion. Copper clad steel conductor wires are a combination of copper and steel, providing a balance of strength and conductivity. Both types of tracer wires are essential for ensuring accurate and efficient detection of pipelines during excavation and maintenance work.</p></p>
