<p><p>SSL certification is crucial for ensuring secure and trustworthy online interactions. There are three main types of SSL certificates in the market:</p><p>1. Domain Validation (DV) SSL Certificate: This basic level of SSL certificate is used to secure domain names. It verifies that the domain owner has control and can receive emails from that domain, offering minimal validation.</p><p>2. Extended Validation (EV) SSL Certificate: This type provides the highest level of authentication. EV SSL ensures the legitimacy of the website by verifying the business's legal existence, physical address, and other requirements, presenting a green address bar to indicate trustworthiness.</p><p>3. Organization Validation (OV) SSL Certificate: OV SSL sits between DV and EV certificates, offering moderate verification. It ensures that the business requesting the certificate is legitimate by verifying relevant details about the company. It provides more trust than DV but less than EV certificates.</p></p>
