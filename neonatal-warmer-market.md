<p><p>A neonatal warmer, also known as an infant incubator or baby incubator, is a medical device used to create a controlled environment for premature or ill infants. It helps regulate the baby's body temperature, providing warmth and promoting growth and development. Neonatal warmers are commonly used in neonatal intensive care units (NICUs), delivery rooms, and hospitals.</p><p>The Neonatal Warmer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.32% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, there is a rising incidence of preterm births worldwide, which drives the demand for neonatal warmers. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative and efficient neonatal warmers, further propelling market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the neonatal warmer market is the increasing adoption of portable and transportable warmers. These devices provide flexibility and convenience, allowing healthcare providers to provide care to premature infants even during transportation. They are particularly beneficial in regions with limited healthcare facilities.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of advanced features in neonatal warmers, such as closed-loop control systems and non-contact thermometers. These features ensure better control of temperature and minimize the risk of infections.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing focus on providing personalized and individualized care to premature infants. This has led to the development of neonatal warmers with options for customization, allowing healthcare providers to adjust settings according to the specific needs of each infant.</p><p>Overall, the neonatal warmer market is expected to experience significant growth due to the rising prevalence of preterm births and technological advancements in the design and functionality of neonatal warmers.</p></p>
