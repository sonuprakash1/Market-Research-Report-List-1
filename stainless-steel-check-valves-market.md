<p><p>Stainless steel check valves are available in two main market types: Y-pattern design and T-pattern design. Y-pattern design check valves are structured with a Y-shaped body, allowing for a straight flow path and reduced pressure drop. T-pattern design check valves have a T-shaped body, offering a more compact design and versatile installation options. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing for their reliability, durability, and efficient flow control capabilities.</p></p>
