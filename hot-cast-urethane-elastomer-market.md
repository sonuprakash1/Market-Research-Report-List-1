<p><p>Hot cast urethane elastomers are divided into three main types based on the type of isocyanate used in their production: TDI-based, MDI-based, and others. TDI-based hot cast urethanes have excellent abrasion resistance and tear strength, making them ideal for applications such as mining equipment. MDI-based hot cast urethanes offer increased durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for applications in the oil and gas industry. Other types of hot cast urethanes include specialty formulations tailored to specific industry needs.</p></p>
