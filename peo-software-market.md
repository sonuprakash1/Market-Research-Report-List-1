<p><p>PEO (Professional Employer Organization) software is available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise PEO software is installed and operated on the user's local servers. It requires upfront investment in hardware and involves maintaining the software internally. Cloud-Based PEO software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It eliminates the need for upfront investment and provides easier scalability and accessibility as it can be accessed from any location with an internet connection.</p></p>
