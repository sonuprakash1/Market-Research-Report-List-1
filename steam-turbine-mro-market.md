Axial turbines have blades parallel to the shaft's axis, Radial turbines have blades perpendicular to the shaft's axis, Mixed turbines have a combination of both axial and radial blades, Tangential turbines have blades at an angle to the shaft's axis, and Helical turbines have twisted blades. Reentry market focuses on the refurbishment and maintenance of turbines used in aerospace and defense applications.</p></p>
