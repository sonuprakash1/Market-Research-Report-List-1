<p><strong>The Automotive In-wheel Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Rotor Type</li><li>Inner Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive In-wheel Motor Market consists of two main types: Outer Rotor Type and Inner Rotor Type. Outer Rotor Type motors have the rotor outside the stator, providing higher torque at low speeds. They are suitable for heavy-duty vehicles and off-road applications. On the other hand, Inner Rotor Type motors have the rotor inside the stator, offering better efficiency and higher speed capabilities. They are commonly used in electric vehicles for better performance and energy efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in advancing the automotive industry towards electrification.</p></p>
