<p><p>The Lithium Battery Ternary Materials Market includes two main types of materials: Positive Electrode Material and Negative Electrode Material. Positive electrode materials, such as lithium cobalt oxide, are responsible for storing and releasing lithium ions during battery operation. Negative electrode materials, like graphite or silicon, allow for the movement of lithium ions in and out of the battery. Both types of materials play a crucial role in the overall performance and efficiency of lithium batteries.</p></p>
