<p><p>Automatic or Autonomous Emergency Braking systems are categorized into three main types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning alerts the driver of a potential collision, Dynamic Brake Support provides additional braking assistance to minimize the impact of a collision, and Crash Imminent Braking automatically applies the brakes when a collision is imminent. These systems work together to enhance vehicle safety by helping drivers avoid or mitigate accidents.</p></p>
