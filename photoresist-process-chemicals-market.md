<p><p>Photoresist process chemicals are used in the manufacturing of electronic components. The market for these chemicals can be categorized into four main types: solvents, binders, sensitizer, and others. Solvents are used to dissolve photoresist materials, binders help in adhering the photoresist to the substrate, and sensitizers improve the sensitivity of the photoresist to light. Other chemicals in this market may include developers, strippers, and anti-reflective coatings. All these chemicals play a crucial role in the successful implementation of the photoresist process in semiconductor and electronics industries.</p></p>
