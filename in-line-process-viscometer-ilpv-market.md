<p><p>In-Line Process Viscometers (ILPV) are used in various industries to measure the viscosity of fluids in real-time. The market types of ILPV include Rotational, Torsional Oscillation, Vibration, Moving Piston, Coriolis, Dynamic Fluid Pressure, and Acoustic Wave (Solid-State) technologies. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for specific applications. Rotational viscometers use a rotating spindle to measure viscosity, while Torsional Oscillation viscometers measure the changes in torque to determine viscosity. Vibration, Moving Piston, Coriolis, Dynamic Fluid Pressure, and Acoustic Wave technologies also have their own methods for viscosity measurement.</p></p>
