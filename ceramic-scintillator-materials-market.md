<p><p>Ceramic scintillator materials are used in various industries like healthcare, security, and nuclear physics for radiation detection. The market for these materials can be classified into two types: Multi Crystal and Single Crystal. Multi Crystal scintillators are made up of multiple small crystals embedded in a ceramic matrix, offering cost-effectiveness and ease of production. On the other hand, Single Crystal scintillators are made up of a single crystal, providing higher resolution and sensitivity, but at a higher cost. Both types cater to different needs and applications within the market.</p></p>
