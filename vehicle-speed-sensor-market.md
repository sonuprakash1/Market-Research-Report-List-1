<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Sensor market includes two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use Hall effect technology to measure the speed of a vehicle by detecting changes in magnetic fields. Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on magnets and electric signals to determine the speed of a vehicle. Both types of sensors play a critical role in providing accurate speed data to various vehicle systems for proper functioning and performance.</p></p>
