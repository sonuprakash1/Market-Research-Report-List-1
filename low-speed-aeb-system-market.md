<p><ul><li>Forward Collision Warning</li><li>Dynamic Brake Support</li><li>Crash Imminent Braking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low speed AEB systems are designed to prevent or mitigate collisions at low speeds. The system includes three main types: Forward Collision Warning, which alerts the driver of an impending collision, Dynamic Brake Support, which increases braking force if the driver does not apply enough pressure, and Crash Imminent Braking, which automatically applies the brakes to prevent a collision. These systems work together to enhance driver safety and reduce the risk of accidents at low speeds.</p></p>
