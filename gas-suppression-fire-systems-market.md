<p><p>Gas suppression fire systems are designed to quickly and effectively extinguish fires in enclosed spaces without causing damage to equipment or the environment. Two main types of gas suppression systems are inert gas fire suppression systems and chemical fire suppression systems. Inert gas systems work by displacing oxygen to suffocate the fire, while chemical systems use specialized agents to chemically react and extinguish the fire. Both types are essential for protecting valuable assets in areas where traditional water-based systems are not suitable.</p></p>
