<p><p>Thin-Film Optical Filters are used in various industries such as telecommunications, medical devices, and aerospace. The market types include Bandpass Filters which transmit light within a certain range of wavelengths, Notch Filters which block specific wavelengths, Shortpass Edge Filters which transmit light below a certain wavelength, Longpass Edge Filters which transmit light above a certain wavelength, and Dichroic Filters which separate light into different wavelengths. Each type of filter serves a specific purpose in manipulating the transmission of light for different applications.</p></p>
