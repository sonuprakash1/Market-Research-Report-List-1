<p><p>Electronic Grade Dimethyl Carbonate Market Types -Propylene Oxide Method and Ethylene Oxide Method are classified based on the production process of dimethyl carbonate. Both methods involve the synthesis of dimethyl carbonate from different starting materials - propylene oxide and ethylene oxide, respectively. The Propylene Oxide Method produces dimethyl carbonate through the reaction of propylene oxide with methanol, while the Ethylene Oxide Method involves the reaction of ethylene oxide with carbon dioxide and methanol. These methods are crucial in determining the quality and purity of the electronic grade dimethyl carbonate produced.</p></p>
