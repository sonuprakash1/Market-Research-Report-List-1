<p><p>The thermoplastic engine cover market consists of two main types: injection molding and casting. Injection molding involves injecting molten plastic into a mold under high pressure to create the desired shape and design of the engine cover. Casting, on the other hand, involves pouring molten plastic into a mold and allowing it to cool and solidify to form the engine cover. Both methods are commonly used in the production of thermoplastic engine covers, each offering unique advantages in terms of cost, quality, and production speed.</p></p>
