<p><p>Blockchain in the pharmaceutical supply chain market refers to the use of distributed ledger technology to enhance transparency and traceability in the industry. Private blockchain involves a network that is controlled by a single organization, ensuring high privacy but limited collaboration. Public blockchain is open to anyone, enabling transparency but sacrificing privacy. Consortium blockchain is a hybrid, allowing multiple organizations to collaborate while maintaining controlled access. Each market type offers different levels of privacy, transparency, and collaboration to meet the diverse needs of the pharmaceutical supply chain.</p></p>
