<p><p>Forestry harvester tires come in three main types: pneumatic tires, solid tires, and polymer-based tires. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide a smooth ride, but are prone to punctures. Solid tires are airless and puncture-proof, making them ideal for rough terrain. Polymer-based tires are a newer innovation, offering a combination of durability and flexibility. Each type has its own advantages and is suited to different forestry harvesting applications.</p></p>
