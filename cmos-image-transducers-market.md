<p><strong>The CMOS Image Transducers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI Technology</li><li>BSI Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS Image Transducers are categorized into two types based on their technology - Front Side Illumination (FSI) and Back Side Illumination (BSI). FSI technology involves capturing light through the front side of the sensor, offering cost-effective solutions for low-light imaging applications. On the other hand, BSI technology captures light through the back side of the sensor, resulting in higher sensitivity and better image quality, making it ideal for high-end smartphones and digital cameras. Both technologies cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
