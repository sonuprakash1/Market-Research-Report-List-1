<p><p>Sound recognition is a technology that enables the identification and interpretation of various sounds by electronic devices or software systems. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of voice-controlled virtual assistants and smart speakers, such as Amazon Alexa and Google Home, has led to a rise in demand for sound recognition technology. These devices rely on sound recognition algorithms to understand and respond to voice commands.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing need for security and surveillance systems has also contributed to the market growth. Additionally, the healthcare industry is incorporating sound recognition for applications like identifying abnormal heartbeats or sleep disorders, further driving market growth.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing the emergence of deep learning-based sound recognition algorithms. Deep learning techniques, such as convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs), are being employed to improve accuracy and reliability in sound recognition systems.
