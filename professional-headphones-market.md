<p><p>Dynamic headphones are the most common type of headphones on the market, utilizing a moving coil driver to produce sound. They are known for their wide frequency range and high sound quality. Moving iron headphones, on the other hand, use a different technology called a balanced armature driver, which offers a more detailed and accurate sound reproduction. Both types of headphones cater to different audiophiles based on their preference for either a dynamic and immersive sound or a more precise and analytical sound.</p></p>
