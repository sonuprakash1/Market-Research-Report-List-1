<p><p>Reactive emulsifiers are used in various industries for stabilizing emulsions. Anionic emulsifiers contain negatively charged hydrophilic groups, making them suitable for oil-in-water emulsions. Nonionic reactive emulsifiers do not have a charge and are versatile, making them suitable for both oil-in-water and water-in-oil emulsions. Both types of emulsifiers play a crucial role in industries such as food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals, by improving stability, texture, and shelf life of products.</p></p>
