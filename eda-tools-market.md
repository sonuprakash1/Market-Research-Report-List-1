PCB/MCM (Printed Circuit Board/Multi-Chip Module) tools aid in designing and testing electronic circuit boards. IC Physical Design & Verification tools help in creating integrated circuits and ensuring their correctness. SIP (System-in-Package) tools enable the integration of multiple chips into a single package.
