<p><ul><li>Striped Structured Light</li><li>Coded Structured Light</li><li>Speckle Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Structured Light 3D Sensing market consists of three main types: Striped Structured Light, Coded Structured Light, and Speckle Structured Light. Striped Structured Light involves projecting a pattern of alternating light and dark stripes onto an object, enabling the capture of precise depth information. Coded Structured Light employs a unique code pattern projected onto the object, facilitating more complex 3D reconstruction. Lastly, Speckle Structured Light relies on the analysis of light scattering patterns caused by surface irregularities, generating accurate depth measurements.</p></p>
