<p><ul><li>Clinoptilolite</li><li>Chabazite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural zeolites market is divided into two main types - clinoptilolite and chabazite. Clinoptilolite is widely used for water purification, agriculture, animal feed additives, and as a soil conditioner due to its high cation exchange capacity. Chabazite, on the other hand, is used in gas storage, catalysis, and as an absorbent for removing heavy metals and radioactive materials from water and soil. Both types of zeolites have unique properties that make them valuable in various industries and applications.</p></p>
