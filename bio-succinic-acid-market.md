<p><p>Bio Succinic Acid Market Types can be categorized into four main processes: Ammonium Sulphate Process, Direct Crystallization Process, Electrodialysis Process, and Others. The Ammonium Sulphate Process involves the fermentation of glucose and ammonium sulphate. The Direct Crystallization Process utilizes microbial fermentation followed by the purification of succinic acid crystals. The Electrodialysis Process involves the separation of succinic acid through electrodialysis. The Others category includes alternative methods of producing bio succinic acid such as bioelectrochemical processes.</p></p>
