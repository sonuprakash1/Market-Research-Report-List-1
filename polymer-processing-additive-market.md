<p><p>Polymer processing additives are chemicals added during the manufacturing of polymers to improve their processing properties. Antioxidants are added to prevent degradation of polymers during processing. Plasticizers are used to increase flexibility and reduce brittleness. Antistatic agents help reduce static electricity buildup. Flame retardants are added to reduce the flammability of polymers. Other types of additives include UV stabilizers, slip agents, and nucleating agents, which help improve various properties of the final polymer product. Each type of additive serves a specific purpose in enhancing the processing and performance of polymers.</p></p>
