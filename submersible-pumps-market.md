<p><strong>The Submersible Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borewell Submersible Pump</li><li>Open Well Submersible Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submersible pumps are classified into two main types, which are borewell submersible pumps and open well submersible pumps. Borewell submersible pumps are specifically designed for use in boreholes or wells. They are efficient in lifting water from deep underground sources. On the other hand, open well submersible pumps are suitable for use in open wells or reservoirs. They are used to pump water from surface water sources. Both types of pumps are widely used in agriculture, irrigation, industrial, and domestic applications.</p></p>
