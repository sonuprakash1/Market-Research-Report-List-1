<p><p>The drug infusion systems market comprises three main types: ambulatory infusion systems, implantable infusion systems, and syringe infusion systems. Ambulatory infusion systems are portable devices that allow patients to receive medication outside of a hospital setting. Implantable infusion systems are surgically placed devices that provide continuous drug delivery over an extended period. Syringe infusion systems use syringes to deliver medication at a controlled rate. These systems cater to different patient needs and are crucial in administering medication efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
