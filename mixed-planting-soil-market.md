<p><ul><li>Peat Planting Soil</li><li>Peat Free Planting Soil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mixed planting soil market is divided into two types: Peat planting soil and peat-free planting soil. Peat planting soil is composed of partially decomposed organic matter (peat moss) which is extracted from bogs. It is known for its ability to retain water and create an ideal environment for plant growth. On the other hand, peat-free planting soil is derived from a combination of organic materials like coconut coir, compost, and perlite. It is considered an eco-friendly alternative to peat soil, as it reduces carbon emissions and protects natural peatlands from degradation.</p></p>
