<p><p>Gasoline recreational vehicles can be categorized into two main types: motorized RVs and towable RVs. Motorized RVs are vehicles that come with a built-in engine and can be driven independently, such as Class A, B, and C motorhomes. On the other hand, towable RVs are vehicles that need to be towed by another vehicle, such as travel trailers, fifth-wheel trailers, and toy haulers. Both types offer unique features and benefits to cater to different preferences and needs of recreational vehicle enthusiasts.</p></p>
