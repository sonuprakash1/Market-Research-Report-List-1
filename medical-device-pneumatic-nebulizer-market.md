<p><p>Medical Device Pneumatic Nebulizer market includes two main types: Vented Pneumatic Nebulizers and Breath-Actuated Pneumatic Nebulizers. Vented Pneumatic Nebulizers require the patient to coordinate their breathing with the device, delivering medication continuously. On the other hand, Breath-Actuated Pneumatic Nebulizers only release medication when the patient inhales, making them more efficient and reducing drug wastage. Both types of nebulizers are commonly used in respiratory treatments for conditions such as asthma, COPD, and cystic fibrosis.</p></p>
