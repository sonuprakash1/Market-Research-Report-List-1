<p><p>The silicone market can be divided into various types including elastomers, fluids & emulsions, resins, and gels. Elastomers are highly flexible and stretchable materials commonly used in products like sealants and adhesives. Fluids & emulsions are liquid silicone compounds used in lubricants and surfactants. Resins are solid silicone materials used in molding and casting applications. Gels are semi-solid silicone products used in skincare and medical applications due to their soft and smooth texture.</p></p>
