<p><p>SiC UV sensors are broadly categorized into three types based on the range of ultraviolet light they can detect: UVA sensors, UVB sensors, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors are designed to detect UVA light, which falls within the range of 320-400nm. UVB sensors detect UVB light, which ranges from 280-320nm. Lastly, UVC sensors are capable of detecting UVC light, which ranges from 100-280nm. Each type of sensor is specifically optimized to provide accurate and reliable measurements within their respective UV ranges.</p></p>
