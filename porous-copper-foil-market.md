<p><strong>The Porous Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Porosity: Under 50μm</li><li>Porosity: 50μm - 100μm</li><li>Porosity: Above 100μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Porous copper foil is classified into three main types based on porosity levels: Under 50μm, 50μm - 100μm, and Above 100μm. Foils with porosity under 50μm offer the highest level of density and are commonly used in applications requiring maximum conductivity. Foils with porosity between 50μm and 100μm strike a balance between conductivity and flexibility. Foils with porosity above 100μm are more flexible and are often used in applications where conformability is required.</p></p>
