<p><strong>The Copper Foil for EMI Shielding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper foil for EMI shielding comes in two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is made by passing copper through a pair of rolls, resulting in a thin and flexible product that is ideal for applications requiring high conductivity and flexibility. Electrolytic Copper Foil, on the other hand, is produced by electroplating copper onto a metal surface, making it a cost-effective option for EMI shielding applications. Both types of copper foil are widely used in industries such as electronics, telecommunications, and automotive for shielding against electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
