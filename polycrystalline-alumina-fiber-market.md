<p><p>Polycrystalline Alumina Fiber market includes two main types: Mullite Fiber and High Alumina Fiber. Mullite Fiber is composed mainly of alumina and silica, offering high temperature resistance and excellent thermal shock resistance. High Alumina Fiber, on the other hand, is made mostly of alumina, providing superior strength and resistance to chemical corrosion. Both types of fibers are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and ceramics for their exceptional properties and durability in high temperature environments.</p></p>
