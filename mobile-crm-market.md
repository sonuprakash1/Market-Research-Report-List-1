<p><p>Mobile CRM refers to customer relationship management software that is accessible via mobile devices. The market for mobile CRM can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CRM is installed and hosted on the company's servers, providing control and data security but requiring maintenance and infrastructure costs. Alternatively, cloud-based CRM is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, with the vendor in charge of software updates and maintenance. Both types enable businesses to manage customer relationships anytime, anywhere using mobile devices.</p></p>
