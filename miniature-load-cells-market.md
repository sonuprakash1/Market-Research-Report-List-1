<p><strong>The Miniature Load Cells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflection Type</li><li>Blocking Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Miniature load cells can be categorized into two main types: Reflection Type and Blocking Type. Reflection Type load cells measure force based on the reflection of infrared light from a moving diaphragm, providing high accuracy and reliability. Blocking Type load cells, on the other hand, measure force by detecting changes in electrical resistance within a semiconductor material, offering a cost-effective solution for smaller applications. Both types cater to the demand for compact and precise force measurement devices in various industries.</p></p>
