<p><strong>The 3D Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Scanners</li><li>Portable CMM Based Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D scanners are specialized devices used to capture three-dimensional data of objects or environments. Laser scanners utilize lasers to measure distances and create precise 3D models of objects. Portable CMM based scanners, on the other hand, use a coordinate measuring machine (CMM) to capture accurate 3D data through physical contact with the object. Both types of scanners are widely used in industries such as engineering, manufacturing, and construction for tasks like quality control, reverse engineering, and creating digital models.</p></p>
