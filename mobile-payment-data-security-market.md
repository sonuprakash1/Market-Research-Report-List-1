<p><p>Mobile payment data security refers to the protection of sensitive information when making mobile payments. Contactless tokenization involves replacing card details with a unique token, ensuring that no personal data is transmitted during the payment process. Remote tokenization, on the other hand, involves storing payment data in a secure cloud environment, allowing users to make mobile payments without exposing their sensitive information. Both market types aim to enhance data security and prevent unauthorized access to payment data during mobile transactions.</p></p>
