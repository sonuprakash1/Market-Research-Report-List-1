<p><strong>The Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Annular Blowout Preventer</li><li>Ram Blowout Preventer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subsea Blowout Preventer (BOP) market includes two main types - Annular Blowout Preventer and Ram Blowout Preventer. Annular BOP is a large rubber seal that can close in a variety of sizes around a drill pipe, while Ram BOP consists of steel plungers that can be hydraulically or mechanically actuated to seal the wellbore. Both types play a crucial role in preventing blowouts during drilling operations by sealing off the wellbore in case of unexpected pressure surges.</p></p>
