<p><p>The eyelash brush market encompasses three main types: comb shape, spiral shape, and bullet shape. Comb shape brushes feature evenly spaced teeth that separate and define lashes, providing a natural look. Spiral shape brushes have curved bristles that coat each lash evenly and add volume for a more dramatic effect. Bullet shape brushes have a tapered tip, allowing precise application to individual lashes. Each brush type offers different benefits, allowing customers to choose the one that suits their desired look and application preferences.</p></p>
