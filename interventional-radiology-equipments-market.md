Ultrasound imaging systems use high-frequency sound waves to produce images of the body's structures. CT scanners use X-rays to generate cross-sectional images. Angiography systems visualize blood vessels, while fluoroscopy systems provide real-time moving images. Biopsy systems help in obtaining tissue samples for further examination.</p></p>
