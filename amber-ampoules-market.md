<p><p>Amber ampoules are available in two main market types: tube made bottle and molded bottle. With tube made bottles, the ampoules are formed by blowing glass tubes into shapes and then sealed. This method offers flexibility in terms of size and shape. On the other hand, molded bottles are created by injecting molten glass into molds, which results in a consistent shape. Molded bottles are preferred for mass production and maintaining uniformity. Both tube made and molded bottles cater to the demand for amber ampoules, providing options to suit different production requirements.</p></p>
