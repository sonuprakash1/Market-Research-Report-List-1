<p><ul><li>Pressure Control Valves</li><li>Shift Control Valves</li><li>Timing Valves</li><li>Pressure Modulating Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic gearbox valves play a crucial role in controlling the pressure, timing, and shifting functions of automatic transmissions. Pressure control valves regulate the fluid pressure within the gearbox, ensuring smooth operation. Shift control valves manage the timing and sequence of gear shifts. Timing valves determine when specific gears are engaged based on vehicle speed and load. Pressure modulating valves adjust pressure levels to optimize performance. Each type of valve is essential for maintaining the efficiency and functionality of automatic gearboxes.</p></p>
