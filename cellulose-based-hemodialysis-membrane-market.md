<p><p>The Cellulose Based Hemodialysis Membrane market consists of two types: Regenerated Cellulose Membrane and Modified Cellulose Membrane. Regenerated Cellulose Membrane is produced by treating cellulose with chemical solvents, resulting in a highly efficient and biocompatible membrane. Modified Cellulose Membrane is created by adding various chemical modifications to the cellulose structure, enhancing its performance in terms of pore size, surface charge, and biocompatibility. Both types of membranes are widely used in hemodialysis procedures due to their ability to effectively remove waste products from the blood.</p></p>
