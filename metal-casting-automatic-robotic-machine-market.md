<p><p>Metal casting automatic robotic machines can be classified into two main types - semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for tasks such as loading and unloading materials. On the other hand, fully automatic machines are capable of operating independently without the need for human intervention throughout the entire casting process. Both types of machines offer increased efficiency, precision, and productivity in metal casting operations, with fully automatic machines being more advanced and requiring less human supervision.</p></p>
