<p><p>Non-Volatile Memory (NVM) market encompasses two main types - Electrically Addressed and Mechanically Addressed. Electrically Addressed NVM utilizes electrical signals for reading and writing data, commonly found in flash memory and EEPROMs. On the other hand, Mechanically Addressed NVM uses mechanical processes such as moving parts or physical changes to store and access data, like optical discs and magnetic tape. Both types play crucial roles in the storage and retrieval of data in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
