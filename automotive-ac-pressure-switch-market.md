<p><p>The Automotive AC Pressure Switch market offers different types to cater to various needs. Dual Function Pressure Switch is used for both high and low-pressure monitoring in the AC system. HPCO (High-Pressure Cut-Out) switch protects the system from overpressure. Fan pressure switches control the operation of the cooling fan. Compressor Cycling switches control the cycling of the compressor, preventing it from overheating. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency and safety of the automotive AC system.</p></p>
