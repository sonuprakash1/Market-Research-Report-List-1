<p><p>Ultrasonic sensors for vehicles come in two main types: closed aperture type and open aperture type. Closed aperture sensors are sealed to prevent any external interference, making them more reliable in harsh environments. Open aperture sensors, on the other hand, are designed with an open face to provide improved detection capabilities, especially in wide-angle applications. Both types are used in the automotive industry for various applications such as parking assistance, collision avoidance, and blind spot detection.</p></p>
