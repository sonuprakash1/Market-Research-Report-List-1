<p><p>OLED encapsulation materials market types include rigid glass, flexible glass, conformal coatings, metal foils, and laminates. Rigid glass offers high durability and protection for OLED displays. Flexible glass provides bendable and lightweight solutions for curved displays. Conformal coatings offer thin and flexible encapsulation for various applications. Metal foils provide excellent barrier properties against moisture and oxygen. Laminates combine multiple layers to enhance encapsulation performance. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in the OLED industry, contributing to the overall market growth.</p></p>
