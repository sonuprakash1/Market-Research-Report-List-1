<p><p>The Unmanned Underwater Vehicles (UUV) market consists of two main types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUV). ROVs are controlled by an operator from the surface using a tether, allowing for real-time control and data retrieval. AUVs operate autonomously, pre-programmed to carry out tasks without the need for constant human supervision. Both types of UUVs are used for various applications such as underwater exploration, research, and defense due to their ability to access deep and remote underwater environments.</p></p>
