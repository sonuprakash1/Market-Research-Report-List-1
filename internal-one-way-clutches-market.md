<p><p>Internal one-way clutches are mechanical devices that allow rotation in one direction while preventing it in the other. There are two main types of designs in the market: roller design and spiral design. Roller design one-way clutches use cylindrical rollers to transmit torque, while spiral design clutches utilize a spring-loaded mechanism to engage and disengage the clutch. Both designs are commonly used in various applications where controlled directional movement is required, such as in automotive transmissions and industrial machinery.</p></p>
