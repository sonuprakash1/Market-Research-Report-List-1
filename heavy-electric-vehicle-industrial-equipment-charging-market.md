<p><p>The Heavy Electric Vehicle & Industrial Equipment Charging market consists of two main types: Static Power Transfer and Dynamic Power Transfer. Static Power Transfer involves stationary charging stations where electric vehicles and industrial equipment connect to a power source to recharge their batteries. On the other hand, Dynamic Power Transfer allows vehicles and equipment to charge wirelessly while in motion, through charging pads embedded in the roadway. Both types of charging technologies play a crucial role in enabling efficient and sustainable transportation solutions for heavy-duty vehicles and industrial equipment.</p></p>
