<p><strong>The Integrated Electric Brake Booster Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Box Solution</li><li>One-Box Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Electric Brake Booster market is divided into two main types: Two-Box Solution and One-Box Solution. In a Two-Box Solution, the electric motor and electronic control unit are separate from the brake booster, offering more flexibility in installation. Meanwhile, a One-Box Solution integrates the motor and control unit directly into the brake booster, reducing the overall size and complexity of the system. Both solutions aim to improve braking performance and efficiency in vehicles while catering to different installation preferences.</p></p>
