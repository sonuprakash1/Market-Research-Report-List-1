<p><p>The eye care vitamins market offers different types of products, including capsules and troches. Capsules are oral supplements that contain vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients beneficial for eye health. They are easy to swallow and are designed to be absorbed in the digestive system. Troches, on the other hand, are a solid dosage form that dissolves slowly in the mouth and delivers the active ingredients directly through the oral mucosa. Troches are preferred by some individuals who may have difficulty swallowing capsules or who want faster absorption of the vitamins.</p></p>
