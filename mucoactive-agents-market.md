<p><strong>The Mucoactive Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Expectorants</li><li>Mucolytics</li><li>Mucokinetics</li><li>Mucoregulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mucoactive agents are medications that help in the clearance and management of mucus in the body. Expectorants are substances that promote the production and expulsion of mucus from the respiratory tract. Mucolytics, on the other hand, work by breaking down and thinning thick mucus, making it easier to cough up. Mucokinetics stimulate the movement of mucus, aiding its clearance. Mucoregulators are drugs that regulate the production and composition of mucus in the body. These different types of mucoactive agents play a vital role in treating respiratory conditions such as cough, bronchitis, and other lung diseases.</p></p>
