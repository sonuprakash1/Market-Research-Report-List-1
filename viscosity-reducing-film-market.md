UV viscosity reducing films are designed to decrease the viscosity of a liquid or solution when exposed to ultraviolet light. On the other hand, thermal viscosity reducing films utilize heat to reduce viscosity. Both types of films offer a convenient and efficient way to control the flow properties of fluids in various industrial applications, making processes more cost-effective and environmentally friendly.</p></p>
