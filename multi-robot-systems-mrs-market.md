<p><p>The Multi-Robot Systems (MRS) market consists of two main types: Cloud-based and On-Premises. In Cloud-based MRS, the software and infrastructure are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the system through the internet. This offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as resources can be shared among multiple robots. On the other hand, On-Premises MRS involves deploying the system within the user's physical location, providing more control and security over the data, but requiring maintenance and hardware investment. Both market types cater to different needs, offering distinct advantages and considerations for users.</p></p>
