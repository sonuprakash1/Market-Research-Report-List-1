Transfer Molded packaging offers enhanced stability and durability. Thermoformed Fiber packaging involves shaping the pulp using heat and pressure. Processed Pulp packaging is crafted from refined pulp fibers, providing a smooth and clean surface finish. These packaging types are commonly used in various industries for their sustainable and protective qualities.</p></p>
