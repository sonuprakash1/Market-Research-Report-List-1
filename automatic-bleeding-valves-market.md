<p><p>Automatic bleeding valves are widely used in various industries and can be categorized into different types based on their function. Heating type valves are designed to remove air and maintain proper circulation in heating systems. Trace type valves are used for precise bleeding of air and maintaining optimal pressure levels. Combined type valves combine features of heating, trace, and rapid types for comprehensive air removal solutions in a variety of applications.</p></p>
