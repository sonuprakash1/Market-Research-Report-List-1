<p><ul><li>Monocrystalline</li><li>Polycrystalline</li><li>And Thin-Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar energy panel market consists of three main types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film. Monocrystalline panels are made from single silicon crystals, offering high efficiency but at a higher cost. Polycrystalline panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, providing a balance between efficiency and cost. Thin-film panels are the most cost-effective option, as they are made by depositing a thin layer of photovoltaic material onto a substrate. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
