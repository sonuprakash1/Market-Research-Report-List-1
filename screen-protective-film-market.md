<p><p>Screen protective film market can be broadly categorized into three types: PET film, tempered glass, and other materials. Tempered glass is a type of glass that is treated with heat and chemicals to increase its strength, providing more robust protection against scratches and impacts. The "other" market includes screen protectors made from materials such as polyurethane or hybrid combinations, offering unique features like self-healing capabilities or enhanced clarity.</p></p>
