<p><p>Smart Glass is a versatile technology utilized in various sectors such as automotive, construction, and electronics. The three main types of Smart Glass in the market include Electrochromic Smart Glass, which changes tint based on electric current; SPD Smart Glass, which uses suspended particle devices to control light transmission; and PDLC Smart Glass, employing a polymer-dispersed liquid crystal film to regulate transparency. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
