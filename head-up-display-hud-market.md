These systems overlay digital information onto the real-world view through the windshield, providing drivers with enhanced visibility and navigation assistance. <p><p>Head Up Display (HUD) Market has two main types - Conventional HUD and AR-Based HUD. </p><p>Conventional HUDs project information such as speed, navigation directions, and other critical data onto the windshield in the driver's line of sight. This type of HUD helps drivers stay focused on the road without having to look away to check instrument panels.</p><p>AR-Based HUDs, on the other hand, use Augmented Reality technology to overlay digital information onto the driver's view of the real world. This advanced type of HUD can provide more detailed and interactive information to enhance the driving experience.</p></p>
