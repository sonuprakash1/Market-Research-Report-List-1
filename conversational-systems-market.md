<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>On-premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conversational systems are categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based conversational systems are hosted on a remote server and accessed via the internet, allowing for scalability, flexibility, and easy deployment across multiple devices. On the other hand, on-premise conversational systems are installed locally on the user's infrastructure, providing them with direct control over data security and customization. Both types serve the purpose of enabling natural language interactions with users, but differ in terms of hosting and management methods.</p></p>
