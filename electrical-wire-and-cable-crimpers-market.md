Insulated crimpers are used for connecting wires with insulated connectors, providing protection from electrical shocks. Uninsulated crimpers are used for connecting wires with uninsulated connectors, primarily in industrial and automotive applications where insulation is not required. Each type of crimper serves a specific purpose in the electrical wiring industry.</p></p>
