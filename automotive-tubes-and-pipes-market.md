<p><ul><li>Welded</li><li>Seamless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive tubes and pipes can be classified into two main types: welded and seamless. Welded tubes and pipes are made by welding strips of metal together to form the desired shape, while seamless tubes and pipes are produced from a solid metal billet that is heated and then pierced to create a hollow tube. Welded tubes are more commonly used in applications where high resistance to corrosion is not required, while seamless tubes are preferred for applications where strength and precision are crucial.</p></p>
