<p><strong>The Liquid Phenoxy Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne Phenoxy Resins</li><li>Solventborne Phenoxy Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Phenoxy Resins are classified into two main types: Waterborne Phenoxy Resins and Solventborne Phenoxy Resins. Waterborne Phenoxy Resins are dispersed in water, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They are commonly used in applications where low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions are required. Solventborne Phenoxy Resins, on the other hand, are dissolved in organic solvents, providing excellent solvent resistance and adhesion properties. They are commonly used in industrial coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
