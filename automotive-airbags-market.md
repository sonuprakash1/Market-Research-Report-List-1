<p><p>The Automotive Airbags Market is segmented into different types including Frontal Airbags, Side Airbags, Curtain Airbags, Knee Airbags, and Others. Frontal airbags are located in the front of a vehicle to protect occupants during a frontal collision. Side airbags are installed on the side of seats to provide protection in the event of a side impact. Curtain airbags deploy from the ceiling to protect occupants' heads during a rollover or side collision. Knee airbags are located below the steering column to prevent leg injuries. Other types of airbags include rear curtain airbags, inflatable seat belts, and center airbags.</p></p>
