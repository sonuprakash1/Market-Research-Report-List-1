<p><p>Medical laser drilling machines are used for precision drilling in medical devices and implants. Three main types are YAG, fiber, and CO2 laser drilling machines. YAG machines use crystal rods to produce high-energy pulses. Fiber machines use optical fibers to deliver laser beams efficiently. CO2 machines use carbon dioxide to generate a longer wavelength laser beam. Each type has its unique benefits and applications in the medical industry, providing high precision and accuracy in drilling processes.</p></p>
