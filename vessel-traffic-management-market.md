<p><strong>The Vessel Traffic Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>INS</li><li>NAS</li><li>TOS</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vessel Traffic Management (VTM) market mainly consists of four types: INS (Integrated Navigation System), NAS (Network Access Server), TOS (Terminal Operating System), and Other markets. The INS systems integrate various navigational tools and aids, providing real-time vessel positions and safety information. NAS enables vessel tracking and communication through network access services. TOS manages the overall operations of marine terminals, including vessel scheduling and cargo handling. The "Other" category encompasses additional technologies and services related to vessel traffic management but not specifically classified under the above three market types. These systems enhance maritime safety, efficiency, and productivity.</p></p>
