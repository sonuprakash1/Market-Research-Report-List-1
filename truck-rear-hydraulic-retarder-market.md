<p><p>Truck rear hydraulic retarders are braking systems that utilize hydraulic pressure to slow down a vehicle. There are two main types of hydraulic retarders in the market - Parallel Hydraulic Retarders and Tandem Hydraulic Retarders. Parallel hydraulic retarders are installed between the engine and the transmission, whereas tandem hydraulic retarders are installed after the transmission. Both types provide effective braking power and help in reducing wear on the vehicle's brake pads, resulting in improved safety and overall performance of the truck.</p></p>
