<p><p>The alcohol additives market can be divided into various types including colorants, flavors, emulsifying and stabilizing agents, antifoaming agents, and others. Colorants are used to give alcohol products a specific color, while flavors are added to enhance taste. Emulsifying and stabilizing agents help mix different ingredients together, while antifoaming agents reduce foam formation.
