<p><p>Rotary Arm Stretch Wrap Machines are available in three main types based on their level of automation: Manual Stretch Wrapper, Semiautomatic Stretch Wrapper, and Automatic Stretch Wrapper. Manual wrappers require operators to manually wrap the film around the pallet, while Semiautomatic wrappers partially automate the wrapping process by rotating the arm around the pallet but still require some manual input. Automatic wrappers are fully automated and do not require any manual intervention, making them the most efficient and productive option for high-volume wrapping operations.</p></p>
