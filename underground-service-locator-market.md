<p><strong>The Underground Service Locator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Location</li><li>Passive Location</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underground Service Locator market can be categorized into two main types: Active Location and Passive Location. Active Location involves the use of specialized equipment that emits signals to locate underground utilities, making it ideal for cases where the exact location is needed. On the other hand, Passive Location relies on detecting existing signals emitted by underground utilities, making it a more cost-effective option for general identification purposes. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficiency in various construction and excavation projects.</p></p>
