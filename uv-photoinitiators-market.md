<p><p>UV photoinitiators are essential components in UV-curable materials such as inks, coatings, adhesives, and varnishes. There are two main types of UV photoinitiators: free-radical type and cationic type. Free-radical type photoinitiators initiate polymerization through the generation of free radicals, while cationic type photoinitiators initiate polymerization through the generation of cations. Each type has unique properties and applications, making them suitable for different UV-curable formulations. The UV photoinitiators market is driven by the growing demand for sustainable and fast-curing coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
