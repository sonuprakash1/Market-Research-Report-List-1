<p><ul><li>Super-Thin Film (below 6μm)</li><li>Universal Film (6-65μm)</li><li>Thick Film (above 65μm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical polyester film is categorized into three main types based on thickness: Super-Thin Film (below 6μm), Universal Film (6-65μm), and Thick Film (above 65μm). Super-thin films are used for applications requiring high transparency and flexibility, while universal films offer a balance between transparency and strength. Thick films are mainly used for applications that require robustness and durability. Each type caters to specific industry needs, providing a range of options for manufacturers to choose from based on their requirements.</p></p>
