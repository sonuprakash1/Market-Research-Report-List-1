<p><p>Bus wheel speed sensors are essential components in the automotive industry for monitoring the speed of the wheels to ensure proper functioning of the vehicle's braking and traction control systems. There are two main types of bus wheel speed sensors - Hall type and magnetic electric type. Hall type sensors use a Hall Effect sensor to detect changes in magnetic fields, while magnetic electric type sensors use a magnetic pickup to generate electric signals. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficiency in bus operations.</p></p>
