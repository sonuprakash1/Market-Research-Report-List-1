<p><ul><li>Metallic Deoxidizer</li><li>Non-metallic Deoxidizer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deoxidizer market consists of two main types: metallic deoxidizers and non-metallic deoxidizers. Metallic deoxidizers, typically made from elements such as aluminum or silicon, are used to remove oxygen from a substance. They work by forming a stable oxide layer on the surface, effectively preventing further oxidation. On the other hand, non-metallic deoxidizers, such as carbon or hydrogen, act by chemically reacting with the oxygen to form a gaseous byproduct that escapes, thus eliminating the oxygen content. Both types play vital roles in various industries to prevent oxidation and enhance product quality.</p></p>
