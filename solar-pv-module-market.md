<p><p>Solar PV modules are commonly categorized into four main types based on the technology used in their production: Monocrystalline Silicon, Polysilicon, Thin-film, and Other market. Monocrystalline Silicon modules have high efficiency rates and longevity, making them ideal for residential and commercial installations. Polysilicon modules offer a balance between efficiency and cost, making them popular in large-scale utility projects. Thin-film modules are lightweight and flexible, suitable for applications where traditional modules may not be feasible. Other market modules include emerging technologies such as organic and dye-sensitized solar cells.</p></p>
