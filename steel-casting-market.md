<p><p>Steel casting market can be classified into three main types based on the composition of steel used: carbon steel casting, low-alloy steel casting, and high alloy steel casting. Carbon steel casting is widely used due to its high strength and impact resistance. Low-alloy steel casting contains additional elements such as manganese, silicon, and copper for improved properties. High alloy steel casting includes elements like chromium, nickel, and molybdenum for enhanced corrosion resistance and heat resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications.</p></p>
