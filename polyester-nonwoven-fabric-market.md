<p><strong>The Polyester Nonwoven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond Method</li><li>Spunlace Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester nonwoven fabric market can be classified into three types based on the manufacturing method: spunbond, spunlace, and others. The spunbond method involves extruding molten polymer through fine nozzles and bonding the filaments to create a fabric. The spunlace method involves high-pressure water jets to entangle the fibers. Other methods may include needle punching or thermal bonding. Each method offers unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different end-use applications in industries such as automotive, healthcare, and agriculture.</p></p>
