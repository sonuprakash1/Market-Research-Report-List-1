<p><strong>The Citrus Juice Finisher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw-Based</li><li>Paddle Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The citrus juice finisher market can be divided into two types: screw-based and paddle-based. Screw-based finishers utilize a rotating screw mechanism to separate the juice from the pulp and other solids. This method is known for its efficiency and ability to handle high volumes. On the other hand, paddle-based finishers employ rotating paddles that agitate the mixture, allowing the juice to be extracted. This method is preferred for its gentler processing, making it suitable for delicate fruits or when maintaining pulp integrity is important. Both types cater to the needs of the citrus juice industry with their distinct advantages.</p></p>
