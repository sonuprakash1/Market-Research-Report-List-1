<p><p>NBR compounding market types include HNBR and XNBR. HNBR, or hydrogenated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber, is known for its excellent resistance to heat, oil, and chemicals, making it suitable for applications in automotive, oil and gas, and industrial sectors. XNBR, or carboxylated Nitrile Butadiene Rubber, offers improved resistance to oil and fuel compared to standard NBR, making it ideal for use in gaskets, seals, and hoses. Both HNBR and XNBR have specific properties that cater to different industries and applications within the NBR compounding market.</p></p>
