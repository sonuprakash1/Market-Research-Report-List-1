<p><p>Jewelry cleaners come in various forms to cater to different cleaning needs. Liquid gel cleaners are solutions that can be applied directly or via soaking to clean the jewelry. Ammonia solution is a popular cleaner for diamonds and gemstones, while ethyl alcohol solution is suitable for delicate gold or silver pieces. There are also other market options available, such as ultrasonic cleaners or steam cleaners, which utilize advanced technology for deep cleaning.</p></p>
