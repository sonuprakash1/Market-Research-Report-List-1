<p><p>Sn-Cu-PET foil is a type of composite material consisting of tin and copper layers bonded together with a polyester film. The market types include rolled copper foil, which is produced by rolling and annealing copper into thin sheets, and electrolytic copper foil, which is made through an electroplating process. Rolled copper foil is known for its flexibility and high conductivity, while electrolytic copper foil is preferred for its high purity and smooth surface finish. Both types are commonly used in electronic applications.</p></p>
