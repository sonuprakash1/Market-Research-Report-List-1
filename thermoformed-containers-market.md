<p><p>Thermoformed containers are segmented into different types based on the material used for manufacturing. These include Polyethylene, Polypropylene, Polyvinyl Chloride, Polystyrene, and others. Polyethylene containers are known for their durability and flexibility, while Polypropylene containers offer high heat resistance. Polyvinyl Chloride containers provide excellent transparency and resistance to moisture. Polystyrene containers are known for their insulation properties. Other materials used in thermoformed containers include PET and Polycarbonate, each offering unique characteristics to cater to specific packaging needs.</p></p>
