<p><ul><li>Release Liner Labels</li><li>Linerless Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure labels market includes two main types: release liner labels and linerless labels. Release liner labels consist of a label face stock attached to a release liner for easy application. These are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products. On the other hand, linerless labels do not have a backing liner, reducing waste and making them more environmentally friendly. They are often used in the retail and logistics industries for price tags and shipping labels.</p></p>
