<p><p>The Small Headed Screw System market consists of two types: Headed and Headless. Headed screws have a visible head that enables easy tightening and loosening. They are commonly used in applications where frequent adjustments are required, such as furniture assembly. On the other hand, headless screws do not have a visible head and are often set flush with the surface for a more streamlined appearance. They are typically used in applications where aesthetics and minimal protrusion are important, like electronics or medical devices.</p></p>
