<p><p>Slow release coated fertilizers are categorized into three main types:</p><p>1. Sulfur coatings: These coatings gradually release nutrients as the sulfur coating breaks down in the soil, providing a slow and steady supply of nutrients to plants over time.</p><p>2. Polymer coatings: These coatings release nutrients through a controlled release mechanism, ensuring a sustained release of nutrients to plants.</p><p>3. Sulfur-polymer coatings: These coatings combine the benefits of both sulfur and polymer coatings, providing a dual mechanism for the slow release of nutrients to plants.</p></p>
