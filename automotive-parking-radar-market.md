<p><p>Automotive Parking Radar market consists of two main types, Forward and Rear View systems. Forward parking radars are installed at the front of the vehicle and help drivers detect obstacles and other vehicles in front of them, aiding in parking and avoiding collisions. Rear View parking radars are installed at the back of the vehicle and serve the same purpose but for objects behind the vehicle. Both types of parking radars use sensors and alerts to provide drivers with assistance in parking and maneuvering their vehicles safely.</p></p>
