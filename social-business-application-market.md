<p><p>Social business applications can be categorized into two main market types: on-premises and cloud. On-premises applications are installed and hosted on the organization's own infrastructure, providing complete control over data security and customization. Conversely, cloud-based applications are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. These applications enable businesses to collaborate, communicate, and efficiently manage various social and business processes. While on-premises applications require more maintenance and upfront costs, cloud-based applications offer ease of deployment, accessibility, and cost efficiency.</p></p>
