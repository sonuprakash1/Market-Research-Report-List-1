<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable projectors come in two main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display). DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to reflect light and create an image, resulting in higher contrast ratios and smoother images. On the other hand, LCD projectors use liquid crystal panels to display images, producing bright and vibrant colors. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, with DLP projectors being favored for their superior image quality and LCD projectors known for their affordability and versatility.</p></p>
