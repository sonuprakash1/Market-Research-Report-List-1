<p><p>The medical/diagnostic imaging market consists of various types of equipment used for visualization and diagnosis of medical conditions. X-ray machines use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the body's internal structures. Computed Tomography (CT) scanners provide detailed images of organs and tissues using X-rays from multiple angles. Ultrasound equipment uses high-frequency sound waves to create images of the body's internal structures. MRI equipment uses a powerful magnetic field to produce detailed images of organs and tissues. Nuclear imaging involves the use of radioactive substances to create images of organs and tissues for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
