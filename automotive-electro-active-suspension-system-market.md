<p><p>Automotive Electro Active Suspension Systems utilize advanced technology to provide a smooth and comfortable ride by actively adjusting the suspension components in response to driving conditions. There are four main types of suspension systems in the market: Air Suspension, Hydraulic Suspension, Electromagnetic Suspension, and Electro-hydraulic Suspension. Air Suspension uses air-filled bellows to support the vehicle, Hydraulic Suspension uses fluid-filled chambers for shock absorption, Electromagnetic Suspension uses electromagnets to control damping, and Electro-hydraulic Suspension combines hydraulic and electronic systems for optimal performance.</p></p>
