<p><p>Bio-based films can be categorized into two main types: compostable films and non-compostable films. Compostable films are made from renewable resources and break down into natural elements in composting environments, reducing waste and environmental impact. Non-compostable films, on the other hand, are also derived from renewable sources but do not break down easily in composting environments. Both types of bio-based films offer sustainable alternatives to traditional petroleum-based films, catering to consumers' increasing demand for eco-friendly packaging solutions.</p></p>
