<p><p>The Spring Type Safety Valve market can be categorized into two types: Closed Type and Non-closed Type. Closed Type valves are designed to fully close the valve after releasing excess pressure, ensuring no leakage. These valves provide high safety and are commonly used in industries where it is crucial to prevent any fluid or gas leakage. On the other hand, Non-closed Type valves do not fully close after pressure release, resulting in some leakage. These valves are typically used in industries where minor fluid or gas loss is acceptable.</p></p>
