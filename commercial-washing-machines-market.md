<p><p>Commercial washing machines refer to large, heavy-duty devices specifically designed for commercial use, such as laundromats, hotels, and hospitals. There are two main types of commercial washing machines: front-load and top-load. Front-load commercial washing machines have a door on the front through which clothes are loaded and tend to be more energy-efficient with higher capacities. On the other hand, top-load commercial washing machines have a door on the top and are better suited for smaller spaces with lower capacities. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently washing a large amount of laundry in commercial settings.</p></p>
