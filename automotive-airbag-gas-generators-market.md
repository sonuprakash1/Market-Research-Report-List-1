<p><p>Automotive airbag gas generators come in various types, including Azide Gas Generator, 5-Aminotetrazole (5-AT) Gas Generators, Guanidine Nitrate Gas Generator, and others. Azide gas generators use sodium azide to produce gas for inflating airbags, while 5-Aminotetrazole generators utilize 5-AT as a gas-forming agent. Guanidine nitrate gas generators create gas through the decomposition of guanidine nitrate. Other types of gas generators may use different chemical compounds or methods to generate gas for airbag deployment in vehicles.</p></p>
