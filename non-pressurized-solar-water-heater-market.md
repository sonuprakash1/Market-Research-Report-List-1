<p><p>Non-Pressurized Solar Water Heater market offers three main types: Evacuated Tube, Flat Plate, and Unglazed systems. Evacuated Tube systems use vacuum-sealed glass tubes to absorb and transfer heat, making them efficient in colder climates. Flat Plate systems use a dark, flat panel to absorb heat from the sun, most commonly used in warmer regions. Unglazed systems are cost-effective and usually used for heating pools or in warmer climates. Each type offers different benefits and is suited for different environments.</p></p>
