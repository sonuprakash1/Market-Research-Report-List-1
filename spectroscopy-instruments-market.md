<p><p>Spectroscopy instruments market is segmented into various types such as Raman Spectroscopy, Fluorescence Spectroscopy, Near Infrared Spectroscopy, Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy, and Others. Raman spectroscopy involves detecting vibrational modes in a sample. Fluorescence spectroscopy measures the intensity of emitted light when a sample is excited by UV or visible light. Near Infrared Spectroscopy analyzes light absorption in the near-infrared region. Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy measures the absorption and emission of infrared radiation. Other spectroscopy types include UV-Vis, atomic absorption, and X-ray spectroscopy.</p></p>
