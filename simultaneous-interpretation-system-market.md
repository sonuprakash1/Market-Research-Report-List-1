<p><p>Simultaneous Interpretation System market includes two main types of equipment: Portable and Stationary. This type of equipment is lightweight, easy to set up, and typically includes wireless technology for flexibility. On the other hand, Stationary equipment is intended for fixed installations in conference rooms, auditoriums, or other large venues. Both types cater to the needs of professional interpreters and users seeking efficient language translation solutions.</p></p>
